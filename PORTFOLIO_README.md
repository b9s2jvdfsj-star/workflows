# Global Video Engine Portfolio

This portfolio showcases the capabilities of the Global Video Engine platform, demonstrating our ability to produce high-end, cinematic technical content for international audiences.

## Features

- **Dark, Minimalist Design**: Professional aesthetic that puts focus on your video content
- **Interactive Video Gallery**: Hover effects and play buttons for engaging user experience
- **Regional Categorization**: Videos organized by target market (USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Global)
- **Project Type Filtering**: Sort by content type (Market Analysis, Technical Restoration)
- **Responsive Layout**: Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing
- **Engine Showcase**: Highlights the technology behind the production

## Videos Included

### Market Analysis Series
- **USA**: Market Analysis - USA Edition
- **UK**: Market Analysis - UK Edition

- **Canada**: Market Analysis - Canada Edition

### Technical Restoration Series
- **Global**: Precision Technical Restoration (featuring high-fidelity tool sounds and professional voiceover)

## How to Use

1. Simply open `portfolio.html` in any modern web browser
2. Use the filter buttons to sort videos by project type or regional market
3. Hover over video thumbnails to see preview overlays
4. Click the play button to watch videos (only one video plays at a time for optimal experience)
5. Videos are sourced from the `/out` directory

## Technical Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps
- **Codec**: H.264 video, AAC audio
- **Audio**: Professional voiceover with high-fidelity ambient tool sounds (where applicable)
